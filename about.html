Soap Shampoo Home About

Hello, I'm Ian Jay and this is my site. I am an artist who has also taken up simple DIY home projects. I started making soap because I wanted to know what I was bathing myself with and I loved the handmade soaps I bought online. As with all my projects, I looked at a bar of natural soap and said, "I can make that!" After a few classes and some trail and error, I made my first batch of great smelling soap.
On this site you will be able to purchase my fragrant creations. You will watch me expand my soaping knowledge as I try new techniques and scents. I hope you come back frequently to see what I'm up to.
Earlier, I introduced myself as Ian Jay so you may be wondering who or what is Jennings-Myers?

In the spirit of crafting and shaping a living from my own hands I named this business after my Great-Grandmother Leah Jennings-Myers. Who shaped, mixed, molded, colored, and raised a generation of men and women whom I greatly admire. She was a market woman and a seller of things wrought by her own hands and it is in her name that I breathe life into this endeavor.
